With You
"WithYou" is an innovative mobile application that transforms the travel experience by offering affordable and convenient services. It connects travelers with local guides of the same nationality and language.
Features
The application also features freelance photographers who capture stunning photos at various landmarks and sites, enhancing the travel experience and creating lasting memories. Additionally, "WithYou" empowers individuals to work as independent tour guides, fostering cultural exchange and supporting the local economy. By connecting travelers with local guides and offering photography services
Our History
"WithYou" will revolutionize the traditional travel agency model, providing a tailored and enriching travel experience. With its rich history and vibrant culture, Mosul is a popular tourist destination for foreigners. In today's travel landscape, traditional reservation methods through travel agencies can be costly and restrict travelers to predefined itineraries, limiting their flexibility to explore and customize their trips. Additionally, travelers often struggle to find someone who can capture their memorable moments through photography. To address these challenges.
Presentation
We present "WithYou," an innovative application designed to make traveling more affordable and convenient. Unlike traditional travel agency reservations, "WithYou" connects travelers with local guides of the same nationality and language. This fosters effortless communication and cultural understanding, as the guides are not only residents but also experts on the destination country. They serve as both tour guides and travel companions, providing an enriching experience tailored to individual preferences, all at a reduced cost compared to traditional agencies. Moreover, "WithYou" goes beyond traditional tour guiding services by offering access to freelance photographers.
